🥚 Phase 1 — Hatchling (Free)
✓
Survive as a Hatchling (Lay/Hatch)
FREE
▼
Spawn into the server as a hatchling egg (or be hatched in a nest). Simply surviving the hatch counts this objective automatically — no extra actions needed.
💡 This is your free objective. Every run starts with 1/5 already done if you hatch naturally.
🌿 Phase 2 — Juvenile (Priority)
✓
Use a Sanctuary
EASY
▼
Fly to a designated Sanctuary zone and rest there. On Gateway, the Water Access Sanctuary (near spawn) is the closest and safest for juvi Pteras.
💡 Do this ASAP after hatching. Sanctuaries are safe zones — no dino can attack you inside.
⚠️ Don't skip this. It's easy, close to spawn, and stacks your count early.
✓
Achieve Perfect Diet
MEDIUM
▼
Eat a varied diet covering all required food categories for your species. For Ptera: fish + small prey (insects, small lizards) + carrion. Keep all diet bars in the green.
💡 Fish near Water Access, hunt insects near Jungle Sector. Build this gradually over your juvi phase.
⚠️ Never let hunger drain fully — broken diet streaks can reset your progress.
✈️ Phase 3 — Sub-Adult (Core grind)
✓
Complete Migration (3 zones)
MEDIUM
▼
Fly through 3 designated Migration Zones across the map. On Gateway: Northern Coast → River Delta → Swamp areas. Each zone registers when you fly through it (no landing required).
💡 Plan your migration route to hit all 3 in one long flight loop — saves time and keeps you moving.
✓
Patrol Your Territory (4 zones)
MEDIUM
▼
Visit 4 Patrol Zones spread across your territory. These are usually coastal cliffs, ridgelines, and elevated areas. On Gateway: Northwest Ridge, West Rail Access cliffs, South Plains, SW Coast.
💡 Combine with migration — you can hit patrol zones on the same loop flight.
✓
Survive 3 In-Game Days
EASY
▼
Simply stay alive for 3 full in-game day/night cycles. Each cycle is roughly 1–2 real hours depending on server settings.
💡 Play passively and avoid fights. Ptera's biggest risk is large carnivores at watering holes — stay airborne when possible.
🤝 Phase 4 — Social (Bonus options)
✓
Form a Group / Bond with Others
SOCIAL
▼
Travel with other Pteras (or compatible dinos) for an extended period. This requires another player to group up with you. The bond registers after spending sufficient time together.
⚠️ Skip this on solo runs. It's the hardest to complete without a friend playing Ptera too.
💡 If you have a server buddy, this can substitute for one of the harder migration/patrol objectives.
✓
Successful Hunt (Prey Kill)
MEDIUM
▼
Kill a qualifying prey animal. For Ptera: fish, insects, and small lizards count. You must deal the killing blow yourself.
💡 Fish near Water Access or River Delta. This is easy to stack while building Perfect Diet.
⚠️ Do NOT kill other Pteras — that counts as cannibalism and can block Prime Elder.
📌 Ptera Notes: As a small dino, your server may require only 4 objectives instead of 5 — verify on your server. Ptera is fish+insect+small prey diet. Ptera may NOT eat other Pteras.
100%
