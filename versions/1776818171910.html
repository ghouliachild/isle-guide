🥚 Phase 1 — Hatchling
✓
Survive as a Hatchling
FREE
▼
Spawn into the server as a hatchling. Simply surviving the hatch counts this objective automatically — no extra actions needed.
💡 Every run starts with 1/5 already done if you hatch naturally.
🌿 Phase 2 — Juvenile
✓
Use a Sanctuary
EASY
▼
Fly to a designated Sanctuary zone and rest. On Gateway, Water Access Sanctuary is closest to spawn — do this ASAP as a juvi.
💡 Sanctuaries are safe zones — no dino can attack you inside.
⚠️ Don't skip this. Easy, close to spawn, stacks your count early.
✓
Achieve Perfect Diet
MEDIUM
▼
Eat a varied diet across all required food categories. For Ptera: fish + small prey (insects, small lizards) + carrion. Keep all diet bars in the green.
💡 Fish near Water Access, hunt insects near Jungle Sector. Build gradually over juvi phase.
⚠️ Never let hunger drain fully — broken diet streaks can reset your progress.
✈️ Phase 3 — Sub-Adult
✓
Complete Migration (3 zones)
MEDIUM
▼
Fly through 3 designated Migration Zones. On Gateway: Northern Coast → River Delta → Swamp areas. Each zone registers when you fly through — no landing required.
💡 Plan one long loop flight to hit all 3 at once — saves time.
✓
Patrol Your Territory (4 zones)
MEDIUM
▼
Visit 4 Patrol Zones — usually coastal cliffs, ridgelines, elevated areas. On Gateway: Northwest Ridge, West Rail Access cliffs, South Plains, SW Coast.
💡 Combine with migration — hit patrol zones on the same loop flight.
✓
Survive 3 In-Game Days
EASY
▼
Stay alive for 3 full in-game day/night cycles. Each cycle is roughly 1–2 real hours depending on server settings.
💡 Play passively, avoid fights. Biggest risk is large carnivores at watering holes — stay airborne.
🤝 Phase 4 — Social (Optional)
✓
Form a Group / Bond with Others
SOCIAL
▼
Travel with other Pteras for an extended period. Requires another player to group with you — the bond registers after enough time together.
⚠️ Skip this on solo runs — hardest to complete without a friend playing Ptera.
💡 Great substitute if you have a server buddy — replaces a harder migration or patrol objective.
✓
Successful Hunt (Prey Kill)
MEDIUM
▼
Kill a qualifying prey animal. For Ptera: fish, insects, small lizards count. You must deal the killing blow yourself.
💡 Fish near Water Access or River Delta — easy to stack while building Perfect Diet.
⚠️ Do NOT kill other Pteras — cannibalism can block Prime Elder.
📌 Ptera Notes: As a small dino, your server may require only 4 objectives instead of 5 — verify on your server. Ptera diet = fish + insects + small prey. Ptera may NOT eat other Pteras.
