HOW MUTATIONS WORK

Pick 1 per slot: Slot 1 at Juvenile, Slot 2 at Sub-Adult, Slot 3 at Adult. Slot 4 is for the Elder system. Tap mutations to mark what you have — combos highlight your best matches.

All Builds
⚡ Prime Elder Only
Slot 1 — Juvenile
🌊
Reniculate Kidneys 🔒 Hidden
Unlocks saltwater drinking — the entire coastline becomes your water source.
Unlock: Drink saltwater until "Fluid Deficient" debuff disappears.
🍖
Efficient Digestion
Reduces food drain ~20%. Less hunting, more objectives.
🦵
Reinforced Tendons 🔒 Hidden
Reduces stamina cost for jumping and launching.
Unlock: Jump 50 times before selecting this slot.
Slot 2 — Sub-Adult
💧
Sustained Hydration
Reduces water drain ~20%. Pairs perfectly with Efficient Digestion.
🦵
Reinforced Tendons (Slot 2) 🔒 Hidden
Same effect if missed Slot 1.
Unlock: Jump 50 times before this slot opens.
⚡
Enhanced Stamina
Bigger stamina pool and faster recovery. Fly longer circuits.
Slot 3 — Adult
👁️
Keen Eyesight
Increased visual range. Spot prey and threats from much further.
🦵
Reinforced Tendons (Slot 3) 🔒 Hidden
Final chance if not grabbed earlier.
Unlock: Jump 50 times before this slot opens.
🛡️
Tough Hide
Increased damage resistance. Survive unexpected encounters.
