HOW MUTATIONS WORK

Pick 1 mutation per slot as you grow: Slot 1 at Juvenile, Slot 2 at Sub-Adult, Slot 3 at Adult. Slot 4 is for the Elder system. Tap mutations to mark what you have — combos will highlight your best matches.

All Builds
⚡ Prime Elder Only
Slot 1 — Juvenile
🌊
Reniculate Kidneys 🔒 Hidden
Unlocks saltwater drinking — opens the entire coastline as a water source.
How to unlock: Drink saltwater until "Fluid Deficient" debuff disappears.
🍖
Efficient Digestion
Reduces food drain ~20%. Less time hunting, more time on objectives.
🦵
Reinforced Tendons 🔒 Hidden
Reduces stamina cost for jumping and launching.
How to unlock: Jump 50 times before selecting this slot.
Slot 2 — Sub-Adult
💧
Sustained Hydration
Reduces water drain ~20%. Pairs perfectly with Efficient Digestion.
🦵
Reinforced Tendons (Slot 2) 🔒 Hidden
Same effect if missed in Slot 1.
How to unlock: Jump 50 times before this slot opens.
⚡
Enhanced Stamina

Slot 3 — Adult
👁️
Keen Eyesight
Increases visual range. Spot prey and predators from much further away.
🦵
Reinforced Tendons (Slot 3) 🔒 Hidden
Final chance if you haven't grabbed it.
How to unlock: Jump 50 times before this slot opens.
🛡️

Increases damage resistance. Survive unexpected encounters late-game.
