HOW MUTATIONS WORK

Pick 1 mutation per slot as you grow: Slot 1 at Juvenile, Slot 2 at Sub-Adult, Slot 3 at Adult. Slot 4 is reserved for the Elder system. Tap mutations below to mark what you have — the combos section will highlight your best matches.

All Builds
⚡ Prime Elder Only
Slot 1 — Juvenile
🌊
Reniculate Kidneys 🔒 Hidden
Unlocks saltwater drinking — opens up the entire coastline as a water source. Best map coverage for Ptera.
How to unlock: Drink saltwater repeatedly until "Fluid Deficient" debuff disappears.
🍖
Efficient Digestion
Reduces food drain rate by ~20%. Less time hunting, more time flying objectives.
🦵
Reinforced Tendons 🔒 Hidden
Reduces stamina cost for jumping and launching. Useful if you're frequently grounded.
How to unlock: Jump 50 times before selecting this slot.
Slot 2 — Sub-Adult
💧
Sustained Hydration
Reduces water drain rate by ~20%. Pairs perfectly with Efficient Digestion — your bars just don't drain.
🦵
Reinforced Tendons (Slot 2) 🔒 Hidden
Same effect — if you missed it in Slot 1 or want to double down on launch efficiency.
How to unlock: Jump 50 times before this slot opens.
⚡
Enhanced Stamina
Increases stamina pool and/or recovery. Lets you fly longer circuits for migration and patrol objectives.
Slot 3 — Adult
👁️
Keen Eyesight
Increases visual range. Spot prey and predators from further away — major survival boost as a small dino.
🦵
Reinforced Tendons (Slot 3) 🔒 Hidden
Final chance to grab this if you haven't yet. Best in Slot 1 but usable here.
How to unlock: Jump 50 times before this slot opens.
🛡️
Tough Hide
Increases damage resistance. Helps you survive unexpected encounters while completing late objectives.
